More Info About My Classes

Marching Band

Every morning, I start my day off with marching band. We start practice at 7 in the morning, continuing into first hour. I am in the trumpet section.

Honors Geometry

After band in the morning, I go to my math class. Geometry is a sub-catergory of math that involves shapes, angles, relative position of figures, and the properties of space.

Spanish 1

Next, I go to Spanish class. Spanish 1 is the beginners level Spanish class and teaches us the basics of the Spanish language.

Some things I've learned to say:

  • how to introduce myself
    • ¡Hola! Me llamo Zachariah. ¿Comó te llama?
  • how to say where I'm from
    • Yo soy de Olathe, Kansas en los Estados Unidos.
  • how to say what I like to do
    • Me gusta comer comida y leer. No me gusta jugar a los deportes.

Fitness Foundations

After Spanish, I have Fitness Foundations. I am taking this because it is required to graduate. Fitness Foundations is a basic PE class. We do different exercises including cardio machines (like treadmils and elipticals), weight lifting, running around the track, and other exercises.

Timber Time

Timber Time is an hour block in the middle of the day to eat, get help from teachers, work on homework, and just talk to friends and hang out. Depending one what's happening in my classes, I might do something different for Timber Time, but typically I start off my getting my food and eating with a group of friends. I then might spend the second half talking to friends or going to the library.

Honors Biology

After Timber Time, I have go to Honors Biology with Mr. White. Biology is the study of life and living things.

English Language Arts 9

Next, I go to ELA. In ELA class we have been learning about Greek Mythogy and the Odyssey.

Web Design

My last class of the day is Web Design. In Web Design, we learn the fundamentals of coding a website using HTML and CSS. This website you're reading is actually an assignment for my Web Design class.
